Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our scale services for retail, F&B, and international clients

How often should I service my weighing scale?
For retail and F&B businesses, we recommend every 6 months for high-usage scales and every 12 months for moderate usage. Regular preventive maintenance prevents unexpected breakdowns.
Do you provide on-site repair in Malaysia?
Yes. Our technicians travel to your location across Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Johor, Penang, and other states in Malaysia.
Do you offer emergency breakdown support?
Yes. If your scale breaks down during business hours, we prioritise emergency calls to minimise disruption to your retail or F&B operations.
Can you perform firmware or software updates remotely?
Yes. For supported models, we can connect remotely to update firmware, install software, renew licenses, and configure your system. This is available to clients in Malaysia and worldwide.
Do you support businesses with multiple branches?
Absolutely. We provide scheduled maintenance and on-call support for supermarket and F&B chains with multiple locations across Malaysia.
Do you provide preventive maintenance contracts?
Yes. We offer monthly, quarterly, and bi-annual maintenance contracts tailored to your business needs. Contact us for a quote.
I'm outside Malaysia. Can you help with software issues?
Yes. We provide remote software services worldwide including firmware upgrades, software installation, license renewal, and PLU management. You only need a stable internet connection.
What scale brands do you support?
We service Dibal, Aclas, Rongta, Wintec, TM-F, Digi, CAS, Mettler Toledo, Ishida, Bizerba, Ashica, and more.
